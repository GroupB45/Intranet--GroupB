About

The Academic grade checker is a web application run by Group B as their software project engineering (CMM004). The group consist of 7 masters student who hold different roles at different levels.The application aims to help students track their university grades, calculate their weighted average and predict what they need to strive for in order to achieve their dream degree classification by simply inputting their grades into our website. Academic grade checker lets students worry less about grade calculating giving them more time to focus on their studies and enjoy their time at university.
